"Deny Mode": Text selection will not work on websites that are on the Deny List.

"Allow Mode": Text selection will only work on websites that are on the Allow List.
"Yes": Every request will open in a new popup window.

"No": Every request will open in the same popup window.
"None": Text selection will work normally.

"Some Key": Text selection will not work, until the "Some Key" is held while making the text selection.
"Yes": Bubble will show up normally upon text selection.

"No": Bubble will not show up, and text selection will automatically open in a popup window.
